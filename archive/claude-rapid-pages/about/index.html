About Andreas

I'm Andreas Nyberg, an Atlassian consultant and ITSM specialist with over 15 years of experience in workflow automation, tooling strategy, and service management transformation.

I've supported global rollouts of 4,000 to 5,000 seats across multiple regions, working with organisations that need measurable improvement without losing control of their operations.

My specialism is the Atlassian service stack: Jira Service Management, Assets (CMDB), and Opsgenie. But more than tools, I focus on the human side of transformation: how teams actually work, what processes need to change, and how to build knowledge that stays after the consultant leaves.

I believe in orchestration over automation. Tools don't solve problems; people who know how to orchestrate tools solve problems. That philosophy guides everything I do.

Philosophy

What I believe

Orchestration over automation

The value isn't in the tool; it's in knowing how to use it. Automation without understanding creates fragile systems. Orchestration creates sustainable ones.

Knowledge transfer as success

A consultant who leaves you dependent has failed. The goal is always capability transfer: your team should be able to maintain, extend, and troubleshoot without me.

Iteration over perfection

"Not quite right yet" is a feature, not a bug. Building iteratively means we catch issues early and adapt to what actually works for your context.

Honest assessment

Not every problem needs a consultant. If your challenge is better solved internally or with different tools, I'll tell you. Trust is built on honesty, not sales.
