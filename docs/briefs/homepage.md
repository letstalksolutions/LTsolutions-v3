# Homepage brief

## Goal
Convert qualified buyers into a short discovery call by communicating clarity, credibility, and a low risk engagement model.

## Audience
IT operations and service management stakeholders who need improvement quickly without disruption, and who want a partner that leaves them independent.

## Primary CTA
Book a short call.

## Page sections
1) Hero
- Headline: outcome led, Atlassian anchored
- Subhead: what gets implemented and why it matters
- Trust signals: rollout scale, years experience, specialisms
- CTA: book a call
- Secondary CTA: view approach

2) Problem and outcome
- 3 to 5 pains buyers recognise
- Matching outcomes and reassurance

3) Services
- 3 to 4 pillars max
- Each pillar includes: what, who it helps, typical deliverables

4) Approach
- Four phases displayed simply with short descriptions
- Explicit focus on knowledge transfer and independence

5) Proof
- Rollout scale, examples of outcomes, tool ecosystem familiarity
- Optional: short anonymised mini case snapshots

6) CTA footer
- Repeat CTA with a low friction contact method

## Acceptance criteria
- A first time visitor can answer in 10 seconds: who this is for, what you do, what happens next
- Mobile first layout, no horizontal scrolling, no overly heavy animations
- Accessibility basics: correct heading order, keyboard focus visible, usable contrast
- Performance: avoid heavy video, optimise images, minimal JS
- No internal tool naming or AI references in public copy
