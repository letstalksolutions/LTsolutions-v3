Privacy Policy

How LT.Solutions collects and uses personal data.

Last updated: 25 January 2026

Who we are

LT.Solutions is a consultancy providing Atlassian service management delivery. This policy explains how we handle personal information collected through our website and communications.

What data we collect

  • Contact details submitted via forms (name, email, company, message).
  • Basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information.
  • Communication history when you contact us by email or LinkedIn.

How we use your data

  • To respond to enquiries and schedule calls.
  • To deliver consultancy services and manage client relationships.
  • To improve our website performance and user experience.

Lawful basis for processing

We process personal data based on legitimate interests (responding to enquiries and running the business), contractual necessity (delivering services), or consent where required.

Data sharing and processors

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers who help operate the website or manage communications (for example, email hosting or form processing).

International transfers

Some service providers may process data outside the UK or EEA. Where this occurs, we use appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy or meet legal obligations.

Your rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and may use analytics tools to understand site performance. You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email hello@lt.solutions.
