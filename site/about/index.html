About Andreas

Hands-on Atlassian delivery

15+ years in workflow automation and ITSM transformation, supporting global rollouts and operational change.

LT.Solutions is led by Andreas Nyberg, an Atlassian consultant focused on Jira Service Management, Assets (CMDB), and Opsgenie. The work centres on building service operations that are reliable, measurable, and owned by internal teams.

Engagements combine deep tool configuration with process design, helping service owners move from reactive workflows to clear escalation paths, automation, and knowledge practices that last beyond the project.

Experience snapshot

4,000–5,000 seat global rollouts across multiple regions.

Deep JSM, Assets, and Opsgenie specialism.

Focus on documentation and handover for independence.
