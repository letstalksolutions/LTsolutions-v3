A four-phase delivery framework

A clear sequence that aligns stakeholders, builds the right tools, and transfers knowledge back to your teams.

Phase 1

Discovery and diagnosis

Understand the current state, operating model, and success criteria before any build starts.

Discovery

Understand context and constraints

Map pain points, stakeholder needs, and the operational reality behind the tools.

Diagnosis

Define outcomes and quick wins

Clarify what success looks like and identify changes that can deliver early value.

Phase 2

Mapping the path

Define priorities, scope the first sprint, and agree delivery milestones with stakeholders.

Alignment

Prioritise what matters most

Translate objectives into a roadmap that balances quick wins with long-term structure.

Plan

Scope the first sprint

Agree deliverables, define dependencies, and set the cadence for collaboration.

Phase 3

Implementation and integration

Configure tools, build automation, and integrate systems with regular, visible progress.

Build

Hands-on configuration

Configure JSM, Assets, and Opsgenie to match agreed workflows and governance.

Integrate

Automation with guardrails

Build integrations, rules, and reporting that reduce manual effort and create clarity.

Phase 4

Knowledge transfer and delivery

Document, train, and hand over so your teams can run the system confidently.

Enablement

Training and playbooks

Create documentation, runbooks, and internal training aligned to roles.

Handover

Confident ownership

Deliver a clean handover so internal teams can operate independently.
